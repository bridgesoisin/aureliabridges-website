Welcome

Welcome. I'm Aurelia Bridges, a psychologist and psychotherapist offering a safe, confidential space where you can explore what's troubling you and find your way forward.

Whatever you're going through — whether it's anxiety, a difficult relationship, grief, low self-esteem, or simply feeling stuck — you don't have to manage it alone. Therapy can offer you the support and clarity you need to make sense of your experiences and begin to live more fully.

A Gentle, Collaborative Approach

My work is grounded in Gestalt therapy, an approach that focuses on what you are experiencing right now — your thoughts, feelings, and bodily sensations in the present moment. Rather than simply analysing the past, we explore how your past experiences may be shaping your life today.

Together, we build awareness — of patterns, of strengths, of possibilities — so that you can make choices that truly reflect who you are and who you want to become.

What to Expect

Safe & Confidential

Everything shared in our sessions remains strictly confidential, within the limits I'll explain clearly.

Non-Judgemental
